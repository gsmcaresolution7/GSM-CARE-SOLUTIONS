Frequently Asked Questions

How much does TV repair cost in Noida? +
TV repair costs vary by issue. Basic repairs like remote, HDMI, or sound issues start from ₹299–₹499. Screen, panel, or motherboard repairs range ₹799–₹3,499. We always give a free diagnosis and upfront quote before any work begins.
Do you offer home visit for TV repair? +
Yes! We offer completely home visits across all Noida sectors. Our technician comes to your home, diagnoses the TV on the spot, and repairs it right there in most cases.at just Rs.250 charge of home visiting.
Which TV brands do you repair? +
We repair the selected TV brands listed on this website — Samsung, Akai, BPL, Croma, Haier, Hitachi, Hisense, Intex, Kodak, LG, Lloyd, Mi, Micromax, Mitashi, Nokia, OnePlus, Panasonic, Philips, Android TV, Sony, Realme and Redmi. If your TV brand is listed, we can help with doorstep repair.
How long does TV repair take? +
Most repairs are completed same-day at your home (1–3 hours). If special parts need to be ordered, we'll inform you upfront and usually complete it within 24–48 hours.
Do you provide warranty on repairs? +
Absolutely. All our repairs come with a 90-day written warranty on both parts and labour. If the same issue occurs within 90 days, we fix it free of charge.

Services FAQ

Can you repair cracked TV screens? +
Yes, we replace TV panels for most brands. The cost depends on the screen size and brand. We source genuine panels and install them at your home. Please call us for a specific quote for your TV model.
Do you use original spare parts? +
We always use OEM-grade or manufacturer-approved parts. We never use cheap local substitutes that fail quickly. Using genuine parts ensures your TV lasts and is why we can confidently offer a 90-day warranty.
What if you can't repair my TV? +
In rare cases where a repair isn't economically viable (e.g., cost exceeds TV value), we'll honestly tell you. The diagnosis visit remains free. We'll never charge you for a repair we couldn't complete.
